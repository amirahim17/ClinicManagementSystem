About Our Clinic

MedicHub’s journey began in 2021 when Dr. Harith Johari opened the clinic in Raub, Pahang, Malaysia with the goal of providing patients with immediate medical attention at any time of day. Though it was first intended to be an outpatient clinic, Medic Hub soon gained recognition for its dependability and practical location, developing into a well-respected healthcare facility in the neighbourhood. The clinic's long history of providing healthcare services was made possible by Dr. Johari's dedication to attending to the medical requirements of Raub's residents.

MedicHub stood firm in its commitment to patient-centred treatment as it grew in popularity. The clinic’s operation was based on effectively scheduling patient appointments and identifying new patients from regular ones as soon as they arrived. While new patients undertook registration and provided necessary personal and medical information, regular patients received rapid attention based on their condition. This methodical approach demonstrated Medic Hub's commitment to quality by ensuring efficient procedures and individualized treatment for every patient.

Furthermore, the clinic’s identity has grown to include involvement, with the clinic actively planning of activities like health education initiatives and community screening days. These activities not only gave people a reason to put their health first but also promoted cooperation and trust between the clinic and the community it served. Moreover, MedicHub established itself as a partner in promoting health and preventative care rather than only a source of healthcare services by actively engaging in outreach initiatives.

For many years, MedicHub has embraced technology developments to further improve its offerings. Through the integration of modern technology for accurate diagnosis and treatment, the clinic enhanced patient care efficiency and accuracy. The clinic used innovation from digital diagnostics tools to electronic medical records to streamline business processes and improve the standard of care given to patients. This devotion to keeping up with medical developments demonstrated MedicHub’s commitment to provide top-notch healthcare services in a quickly changing healthcare environment.

Today, MedicHub becomes the living example of the vision of its creator and the combined hard work of its committed employees. Based in technological innovation, patient-centred care and a long tradition of community service, the clinic remains a reliable source of healthcare in Raub and beyond. MedicHub is steadfast in its dedication to its goal of delivering high quality healthcare services that are easily accessible while also ensuring the wellbeing of the communities it serves.

Mission: High quality healthcare anytime for the people of Raub.

Vision: To be the leading provider of 24-hours medical care services in Raub
